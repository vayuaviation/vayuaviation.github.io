Services

01

GSSA

Being a General Sales & Service Agent (GSSA) entails acting as the representative of airlines, facilitating the commercialization of air freight capacity, and overseeing intricate local operations and administrative services. In the realm of air transportation services, airlines commonly collaborate with freight forwarders and handling agents (GHAs) to cater to the needs of shippers.

02

Sales and Marketing

We deploy dedicated Sales and Marketing teams for each of our partner carriers. Our experienced and qualified staff, well-versed in IATA procedures, fares, and ticketing, operate from various stations and locations across India.

03

Passenger and Cargo Handling

We provide Terminal/Commercial/Ramp supervision staff to our partner carriers, particularly those operating online in India. Our personnel oversee GHA activities, execute pre- and post-flight duties, manage aircraft movement handling and passenger traffic, supervise cargo operations (palletization, ULD management, loading/unloading), and ensure smooth transit of import cargo to warehouses.

04

Revenue Accounting

Our comprehensive accounting package encompasses the collection of funds from passenger and cargo sales, consolidation, and transfer to the principal carrier's bank account. We also provide Revenue Accounting Services, such as sales reports, load and revenue reports, and other accounting data tailored to our principal's requirements.

05

Supervision at Airports

Our company ensures effective handling supervision at airports for both passenger and cargo activities. Our dedicated staff monitors ground handling agents to facilitate timely aircraft turnaround and superior passenger services. This supervision extends to both landside and airside operations at the passenger terminal, cargo complex, and warehousing facilities.

06

Interline Negotiations

We take the initiative to negotiate special prorate agreements (SPA) for passenger and cargo carriage with interline partners in India on behalf of our principal carriers. These negotiations are conducted with utmost confidentiality, aiming to secure maximum benefits for our partners.

07

Publicity

We collaborate with our advertising agency, which enjoys a strong industry reputation in India, to execute publicity campaigns as desired by our principal carriers. All promotional activities adhere to the worldwide corporate and product identities of our partners.

08

Sales Marketing

Our experienced and motivated sales teams cover all regions, employing innovative strategies to continually raise awareness of our partner airlines in the market. We maintain close relationships with key trade publications through press releases and advertising, providing weekly and monthly reporting.

09

Operations

Our efficient and experienced cargo operations staff liaise with transit and destination stations, keeping customers updated with delivery details. We possess extensive expertise in handling freight at any airport in India.

10

Finance & Administration

Our services encompass the distribution and control of revenue documents, preparation/conciliation of customer service requests (CSR), collection of payments, reconciliation of accounts, and remittance of funds to airlines.

11

Air Cargo

Our dedicated cargo teams focus on optimizing the utilization of capacity for online carriers by supervising the loading and unloading of pallets and containers. Over the years, we have built a well-segmented database of export houses, shippers, cargo agents, etc., which is utilized by our cargo sales and marketing teams to promote the airlines we represent in India.
